# BASICS 阶段

---

## 1️⃣ BASICS × Guest Service & Safety

### Trainer Intro

> Today we are focusing on how guest service and safety support learning in the basics phase.
> At this stage, students are often unfamiliar with equipment and may feel anxious.
> Creating a safe and supportive environment allows students to become comfortable and mobile.

---

### Instructor Role

> Today we are going to focus on getting comfortable moving around with one foot attached.
> Our goal is to develop confidence using equipment safely.

Lesson Plan:

* Equipment orientation
* Neutral stance demo
* Skating drill
* Safe falling practice

---

### Trainer Wrap

> Notice how safety and comfort increased student willingness to explore movement.

---

---

## 2️⃣ BASICS × Communication & Lesson Structure

### Trainer Intro

> Today we will explore how clear communication and structured lessons support early learning.

---

### Instructor Lesson

Explain neutral stance

Check understanding
