# ✅ D. Trainer ↔ Instructor 角色转换话术大全

这个是 L3 distinction 核心

---

## 明确转换句（必背）

### Trainer → Instructor

```
Now I will switch into instructor role and deliver this as a student lesson.
```

---

### Instructor → Trainer

```
I will now switch back into trainer role to reflect on the teaching skill.
```

---

---

## Trainer 语言特点

Meta level
Instructor development

关键词：

* teaching strategy
* lesson delivery
* instructor application

---

---

## Instructor 语言特点

Student centered
Movement outcome

关键词：

* riding improvement
* student goal
* terrain use
